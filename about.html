About Us

Heba Logistics is a leading logistics and cargo handling service provider committed to delivering comprehensive, reliable, and efficient solutions across the supply chain. With deep expertise in container, vehicle, and general cargo handling, we cater to both domestic and international clients, ensuring safe, compliant, and timely operations.

Our Strategic Approach

By utilizing structured operational workflows and expert strategic oversight, we seamlessly coordinate container logistics, vehicle transit, and intricate heavy cargo arrangements. We focus closely on minimizing processing timeline delays, managing complex regulatory cross-border procedures, and providing modern storage networks.

Our operational presence at major transit points—including Jeddah Islamic Port—allows us to deploy rapid physical support assets directly where client cargo enters and exits.
